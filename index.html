Calculation Methodology

Our Cost of Delay calculation uses a transparent, conservative approach based on established financial principles:

Monthly Profit = Revenue Impact × Profit Margin
Direct Loss = Monthly Profit × Delay Period
Opportunity Cost = Σ(Monthly Profit × (1 + Growth Rate)^month) for each month

This methodology accounts for both immediate revenue loss and the compounding effect of delayed market entry or feature deployment. The calculation assumes linear growth and constant market conditions, providing a conservative baseline estimate.

Important: This tool provides estimates for planning purposes. Actual business impact may vary based on market conditions, competition, and implementation factors not captured in this model.
